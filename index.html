About Me

Kevin Murtaugh is a deeply experienced semiconductor professional adept at solving design challenges for Fortune 500 firms. Kevin has undertaken Technical Sales, Operations, Business Development and even Technical Marketing roles to drive company growth.

Rugby player across five decades, stepped up to local club- and then state-level management roles. At the club level, added corporate sponsorships, IRS 501(c)3 certification, social media and advertising plus professional-level coaching to raise the club's level. At the state level, added metrics for revenue, recrtuitment, travel miles and drove the return of divisional play, all as tools for Florida to improve its national ranking.

Proud parent of a young lady who is tops in her UCF Computer Science curriculum and an officer of both Theta Tau engineering /service fraternity and Society of Sales Engineers. These achievements are merely prelude to what will undoubtedly be her sterling professional career, which starts at Microsoft /Seattle, Summer, 2018.
